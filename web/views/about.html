About
the company
that's innovating
light therapy

Penta Medical is developing fabrics that deliver infrared therapy to your injuries to heal you faster.

By combining our unique infrared-reflective nanoparticles with active light sources, our products save you the time, money, and pain of having to go to a clinic to receive therapy. The products are designed for use by humans and animals, and rely on safe, proven technology to ensure that you recover as quickly as possible.

Infrared therapy is one of the most effective treatments for musculoskeletal injuries.
