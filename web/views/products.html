Products

Our versatile products are tailored for human and equine applications. To use them, simply wrap the fabric around the injured area, specify your injury type, and go about your day.

Human Applications

Athletics | Osteoporosis | Rheumatoid Arthritis

Penta Medical products are designed to be applied externally to any injury on the body while being comfortable, safe, and effective for the patient.

By delivering infrared therapy from a wearable format, our products eliminate the need for you to travel to a clinic to receive treatment. Our wraps are safe to use at home, at work, and everywhere in-between.
